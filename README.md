# Activity Tracker

A comprehensive daily activity and symptom tracking web application.

## Features
- Track activities in 30-minute increments from 6am to 1am
- Morning and evening symptom logging
- Color-coded exertion levels
- Daily notes
- Export to CSV
- Hourly notifications
- Works offline as a Progressive Web App

## Usage
Simply open the app in your browser and start tracking!

## Installation on iPhone
1. Open the link in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with up arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. The app will work like a native app!

## Privacy
All data is stored locally in your browser. Your information never leaves your device.
